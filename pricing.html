Rather than focusing purely on your pace or distance, we take a more holistic approach by emphasizing your mental fitness as well. Running is not a purely physical activity and we believe it’s time that fitness apps reflect that! By combining key principles from mindfulness and cognitive behavioral psychology, we aim to transform the way runners approach goal setting and empower them to develop a more healthy and self-compassionate mindset around their fitness without compromising on performance and drive.

Mindfulness is a loaded and often misunderstood term, but simply refers to the process of focusing on the present moment and acknowledging your inner thoughts and feelings without judgement. Mindful running is the act of applying mindfulness principles to be more aware of what’s happening in your mind, body, and environment while you are running. Mindful running is a powerful tool to develop more self awareness and mental clarity, specifically as it relates to noticing and reframing negative thoughts and self-talk during your runs. Similarly, cognitive behavioral therapy refers to the process of challenging underlying negative thought patterns and beliefs in order to drive sustainable long-term change. Through combining these two principles, we aim to empower runners to better achieve their goals by transforming the way they think about themselves and their fitness journey.
